How many times have you unlocked your phone?

Did anyone ask for this? No. Did we still make it? Yes! Ever wondered how many times you unlock your phone? Why wonder when you can find out with counter! counter adds a module to your control center, that shows you how many times you've unlocked your device. To reset the counter, just tap on it. Simple, yet elegant.

How to use: Simply go into Settings > Control Center > Customize and enable counter from there

Additional Info & Contact

  • counter is also open source! Be sure to check out the code if you're interested.
  • In case you encounter a bug, please create an issue here.
